Questions

The straight answers

What exactly is a "lead"?

A family that found your team or event on Flag Football Finder and reached out — you get their name, contact info, and what they're looking for, straight to your inbox.

Why is Free capped at one lead?

Free gets you listed and seen. Once families start reaching out regularly, Grow unlocks every one of them — most orgs upgrade the first time they hit the cap mid-season.

What makes Pro worth it?

If you run multiple camps, tournaments, leagues, or private training programs, Grow's single event listing won't cut it. Pro lists them all, features them, and comes with onboarding to get the most out of every one.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes on monthly. But annual plans save you ~15% and mean you never lose your placement when your season quiets down — which is when most orgs regret cancelling.
