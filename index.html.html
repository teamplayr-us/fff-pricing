Questions

The straight answers

What is the difference between Free and Pro?

Free lists your programs and sends you one athlete lead a month. Pro unlocks every lead we send, plus a lead dashboard, contact info and export, and athlete inquiry notifications.

What counts as a lead?

An athlete or family that found your program on Flag Football Finder and reached out — name, contact, and what they're looking for, straight to you. Free includes one a month; Pro unlocks all of them.

Do you guarantee leads?

We can't promise a specific number — lead volume depends on search demand in your area and how complete your listings are. What we do is make you visible to real families actively searching for flag football near you, and Pro is month-to-month, so you're never locked in if it's not the right fit.

Can private trainers use Flag Football Finder?

Yes. Trainers and training academies can list clinics, skills sessions, and private training, get discovered by searching families, and capture leads exactly like clubs and camps do.

Do I have to use your registration?

No. Registration is available on both plans but it's optional. You can link out to your own registration page, or connect Stripe and collect payments through us so everything stays in one place.

What does a Growth Campaign include?

A campaign puts your registration window in front of the Flag Football Finder audience through targeted email, social promotion, and platform placement — with segmentation, trackable links, and performance reporting. Campaigns start at $500.

Can tournament and league operators still list programs?

Yes. Tournaments and leagues are searchable inventory on Flag Football Finder, and we can help promote registration windows and reach athletes. We don't replace your tournament-management software — reach out for custom listings and distribution.

Can I cancel Pro?

Yes. Monthly Pro can be cancelled anytime. The annual option is billed yearly at a lower monthly rate. There's no long-term commitment to get started.

What if I operate in multiple cities or states?

We can expand your discovery across markets and support custom listings, distribution, data integrations, and reporting. Use "Talk to our team" and we'll map it to how you operate.
